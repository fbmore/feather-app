Feather

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 29 May 2026

Feather is built so your posture data stays yours. The app processes everything on your device and, if you use iCloud, stores it only in your own private iCloud account. We never see it.

In short  No accounts. No tracking. No analytics. No advertising. Your posture data never reaches our servers — because we don’t have any.

What Feather works with

Feather monitors your neck posture using motion sensors in compatible AirPods (via Apple’s CoreMotion framework). This motion data is read and analyzed entirely on your device, in real time. Raw motion is used to compute a neck angle and a posture state, and is not stored or transmitted in raw form.

What is stored

Feather keeps a record of your posture over time so it can show your history and trends. This consists of daily summary information only — per-day posture composition (good / marginal / poor / critical), angle averages, and total monitoring time. Real-time, moment-to-moment readings are held only briefly in memory while you’re tracking and are never stored or uploaded.

These daily summaries are stored locally on your device. If you’re signed in to iCloud, they are also synced to your own private iCloud account (Apple’s CloudKit private database, container iCloud.com.fbmore.Feather2) so your history appears across your devices and is restored if you reinstall the app. This is the same private iCloud that holds your other apps’ data. The developer of Feather has no access to it, and it is never placed in any public or shared database.

What we do not collect

  • No account, sign-in, name, email, or phone number is required or collected.
  • No analytics, usage tracking, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs of any kind.
  • No location data.
  • No data is sent to the developer or to any third-party server (other than as described below for purchases).

Subscriptions & purchases

Feather offers an optional premium subscription. Purchases are processed by Apple and managed through RevenueCat, which handles subscription status and receipt validation. Feather identifies your subscription using an anonymous identifier generated per installation — no personal information (name, email, etc.) is sent. Apple’s standard purchase terms and privacy practices apply to the transaction itself.

Notifications

Feather can send you local notifications (generated on your device) to remind you when your posture has needed correcting for a while. These are not push notifications and do not involve any server.

Children’s privacy

Feather is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from them.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new “last updated” date.
