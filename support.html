Getting started

Feather works with AirPods that have motion sensors (AirPods Pro, AirPods 3rd generation and later, AirPods Max). Put your AirPods in, open Feather, and it begins tracking your neck posture automatically.

I’m not seeing any posture readings

  • Make sure your AirPods are connected and in your ears.
  • Confirm Feather has Motion & Fitness permission (Settings → Privacy & Security → Motion & Fitness → Feather).
  • Try removing and re-inserting your AirPods.

My history isn’t syncing across devices

Posture history syncs through your private iCloud. Make sure both devices are signed in to the same Apple Account with iCloud enabled. Only completed days sync (today’s in-progress data stays local until the day ends), and syncing happens shortly after you open or background the app.

Managing your subscription

Feather is free to start with a 7-day full-access trial. Premium ($4.99/month or $9.99/year) unlocks full history and weekly trends. You can manage or cancel anytime at apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions — cancellation stops auto-renew, and access continues until the end of the current period.

Privacy

Your posture data stays on your device and in your own private iCloud. See our Privacy Policy for details.
